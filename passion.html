Passion Fruit & Mango-Mint Lassi

Price: R39.99

Serve: 1 persom

Ingredients

2 cups flesh of champagne mangos
3 passion fruits
1/4 cup yogurt
1/4 cup coconut milk
6 mint leaves
1 handful ice cubes
1 tablespoon honey (optional)


Extra servings




Write Up

  1. With the exception of the honey and passion fruit seeds, place all of the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Taste for sweetness and add honey if needed. Divide between two glasses. Add the passion fruit seeds on top and serve immediately.
